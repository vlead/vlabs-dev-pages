Welcome to the Virtual Labs Engineering and Architecture Division (VLEAD) career opportunities. We are looking for competent and enthusiastic people who can bring innovative ideas in design, development and other activities in the virtual Labs project.

Virtual Labs is a mission mode project initiated by the Ministry of Human Resources and Development (MHRD). The objective of this project is to provide laboratory learning experience to the students who do not have access to adequate laboratory infrastructure. Currently there are around 150 labs which have been developed by various institutes.
